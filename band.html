CD Releases + Reviews

The Prodigal
I really enjoyed the Prodigal. It has a mix of upbeat songs and slow but meaningful songs. I also really liked the Bibilicl themes that were represented throughout. My favorite song was "Things That Matter" and "Take Me Home".
Mt. Zion
Mt. Zion was exactly like The Prodigal in the sense that I absolutely loved it. I loved the songs "Slow Down Please" and "The Meaning Of Life"
JESUS IS COMING BACK SOON
This was a single release so it was only one song. However, this one song was absolutely amazing. I loved how Josiah Queen collabed with Forrest Frank. The song was so good.
